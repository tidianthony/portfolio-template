Starter Portfolio Template

A simple, modern design for developers and creatives

About

This is a ready-made portfolio website template that anyone can customize to showcase their work. It’s designed with simplicity, responsiveness, and clean visuals in mind — perfect for developers, designers, and freelancers.

Features

  • Responsive Layout
  • Clean HTML/CSS Structure
  • Customizable Sections
  • Fresh Color Palette
  • Modern Typography

Use Cases

This template can be used as a starter for personal portfolios, freelancer landing pages, or even a small business introduction site. Simply replace the content with your own, and you’re ready to go.
